### Highlights & Notes
- **==Mascot logos==** are logos that picture a mascot as a symbol. In most cases this mascot is either an animal or person that is visually simplified to make it easily recognizable and reproducible.
<!--SR:!2024-05-26,1,230-->

_Some famous examples of mascot logos include the logos of:_
- **KFC**
- **Pringles**
- **Monopoly**
- **Quaker**
