### Highlights & Notes
- In logo design **==a wordmark==** is quite similar to **a lettermark**. Both of these logo types use just the letters of the name of a brand to create a unique and recognizable icon.
- A good example of this is **the FedEx logo**
<!--SR:!2024-05-26,1,230-->

_Some famous examples of wordmark logos are the logos of:_
- **Google**
- **Coca-Cola**
- **FedEx**
- **Sony**
