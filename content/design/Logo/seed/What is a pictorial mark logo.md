### Highlights & Notes
- In logo design, **==pictorial marks==** are logos that display a pictorial depiction of something or someone. In most cases, this can also be seen as a symbol.
- pictorial marks
- are a **great option for smaller logo formats like apps**.
<!--SR:!2024-05-26,1,230-->

_Some famous examples of pictorial marks include the logos of:_
- **Apple**
- **Shell**
- **Twitter**
- **Domino's**
