### Highlights & Notes
- In logo design, **==a combination mark==** is one of the most used types of logo designs. As the name suggests, **it simply is a combination of two, or, in some cases more, types of logo design types.**
- for example, can be used in full on a letterhead or, partially (the abstract mark that is made up of four colored squares), as a small app-icon.
<!--SR:!2024-05-26,1,230-->

_Some famous examples of combination marks include the logos of:_
- **Microsoft** (a combination of an abstract mark and a wordmark)
- **Rolex** (a combination of a pictorial mark and a wordmark)
- **Target** (a combination of an abstract mark and a wordmark)
- **WWF** (a combination of a pictorial mark and a lettermark)
