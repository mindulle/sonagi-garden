### Highlights & Notes
- In logo design, **==a lettermark==** is a logo that is mostly made up of one or more letters that may be modified or made using existing typefaces.
- One of the most famous examples that will make this clear is **the CNN logo.**

_Some famous examples of lettermarks include the logos of:_
- **CNN**
- **IBM**
- **NASA**
- **H&M**
