### Abstract logo
Straying from pictorial representation, an [abstract logo](https://www.wix.com/logo/maker/styles/abstract) makes use of geometric shapes to fashion an image that is truly unique. Over time, this arbitrary symbol will become interchangeable with your brand.

Apart from being one-of-a-kind, an abstract logo will open the doors for global companies, whose audience won’t have to worry about catching localized references or reading text in their non-native language.
