### Orange logos

  

[Orange logos](https://www.wix.com/logo/maker/colors/orange-logo-designs) are vibrant and eye-catching, often used for brands that want to make a bold statement. Orange is associated with energy, enthusiasm, and optimism, making it an ideal color choice for businesses looking to project a sense of confidence and growth. It can be used to create designs that are both playful and sophisticated. Famous brands using orange in their logos include MasterCard, Nickelodeon and Amazon.
