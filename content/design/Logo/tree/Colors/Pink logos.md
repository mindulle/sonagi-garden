### Pink logos

  

[Pink logos](https://www.wix.com/logo/maker/colors/pink-logo-designs) can be an excellent choice for a variety of design projects. Pink is often seen as a playful, lighthearted color which conveys warmth, femininity, and romance. It is also associated with youthfulness, creativity, and energy. As such, pink logos can be used to create designs that are both modern and inviting. Some of our favorite pink logos include Barbie and Pepto Bismol.
