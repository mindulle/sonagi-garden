### Blue logos

  

[Blue logos](https://www.wix.com/logo/maker/colors/blue-logo-designs) can be an excellent choice for a variety of design projects. Blue is often seen as a calming, cool color which can evoke feelings of trust and loyalty. It is also associated with intelligence and professionalism, making it an ideal choice for business or corporate logos. Some striking examples of the use of blue in designing a logo, include Pepsi, Phillips and Procter and Gamble.
