### Purple logos

  

[Purple logos](https://www.wix.com/logo/maker/colors/purple-logo-designs) can be an excellent choice for a variety of design projects. Purple has long been associated with royalty, luxury and sophistication. It is also seen as a color that sparks creativity and imagination, making it a great choice for brands looking to create designs that are both elegant and whimsical. Some of the most iconic purple logos include Cadbury, Hallmark and Yahoo.
