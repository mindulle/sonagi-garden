## ==About Page==

This is a web page that allows visitors to get quickly informed about the company, product, or person behind the website. It is an important part of strong branding and the shortcut to telling the audience about the benefits of what they can find on this website or do with its help, so when visitors want to get a concise introduction of the brand they look for this page. The link to **==About page==** can often be found in the website [header](https://blog.tubikstudio.com/best-practices-for-website-header-design/) or footer, or both.
