## ==Menu Page==

This page is usually about navigation goals. As well as a typical menu control, **==the menu page==** presents a sort of list giving access to different content categories and interaction zones. It is not always needed: the websites with simple structure often use just **==the menu controls==** integrated into the web layout that doesn’t require transitions to a separate page. However, **==the separate menu page==** allows designers to focus the user’s attention on the options without distractors around. What’s more, in many cases **separate menu page** supports the [minimalistic](https://blog.tubikstudio.com/lean-and-mean-power-of-minimalism-in-ui-design/) approach for other pages.
